We are Soley Advisory Group

Soley Advisory Group Ltd comprises a diverse team of seasoned professionals, collectively bringing over 100 years of expertise across various fields. Our mission is to provide strategic advisory services to companies seeking to establish a strong presence in West Africa. We offer tailored solutions, leveraging our deep understanding of the region's market dynamics to help businesses navigate challenges and seize opportunities. From market entry strategies to business development and regulatory compliance, our goal is to support our clients in achieving their strategic objectives and thriving in the West African market.

Our Key Offerings

Infrastructure

SAG provides expertise in developing and managing infrastructure projects, from road construction to utilities, to support sustainable development and economic growth in West Africa.

Financing

SAG offers tailored financing solutions, helping clients secure funding for projects and expansions, ensuring capital is available to drive business success and innovation.

Energy

SAG specializes in energy solutions, including solar, nuclear, hydro, wind, and geothermal, guiding clients through project development, regulatory requirements, and implementation in West Africa.

Agriculture

With a focus on agribusiness, SAG assists companies in agricultural innovation, especially in cocoa production, processing, and export, supporting value chain development for local and international markets.

Public Policy

SAG provides policy advisory services, working with clients and governments to navigate regulatory landscapes, advocate for beneficial policies, and create frameworks for sustainable business operations.

Gold Mining

SAG supports gold mining and refining initiatives, offering expertise in project planning, compliance, and market access to optimize resource extraction and local economic impact.

Transportation
